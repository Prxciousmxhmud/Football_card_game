# Football_card_game
Play a game of football against a computer using a deck of cards
credits for game: https://web.archive.org/web/20080811223241/http:/home.swipnet.se:80/~w-15221/soccer.htm

Instructions: Enter the formation you want from the list of prompts 
- select a player from the row provided to go head-to-head against with a player from your personal arsenal (library) 
- Text prompts will be given to inform you of your progress through the game
- game play will cycle through you and the computer playing alternating when one of you loses possession until a goal is scored or either of your librarys are empty. 

Best of luck and for more information on the game click the above link
